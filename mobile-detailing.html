The core service

What's Included

Mobile detailing is the foundation of everything we do at SB Autoworks. Instead of dropping your car off at a shop and rearranging your day around a pickup window, we bring a fully-equipped mobile setup directly to your home or workplace in Vineland, Bridgeton, Millville, Glassboro, and the rest of Cumberland County — no water or electricity hookup required on your end.

Every mobile detail starts with our Refresh, Signature, or Elite package (priced from $95–$265 depending on vehicle size and condition), and can be built out with add-ons like pet hair removal, odor elimination, or engine bay cleaning.

A Signature Detail Typically Includes

  • ✓ Full exterior hand wash, dry & tire shine
  • ✓ Interior vacuum, wipe-down & window cleaning
  • ✓ Door jamb detailing
  • ✓ Dashboard, console & trim conditioning
  • ✓ Spray wax or sealant for added shine

Want ongoing protection instead of a one-time clean? Our maintenance plan locks in a discounted rate for recurring mobile details — up to 40% off every visit.
