Got questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before booking. Don't see your question? Just reach out.

Do you need access to water or electricity at my location?
Nope — we're fully self-contained and bring everything we need. That said, if you'd like to supply your own water we're happy to offer a discount for that. Just let us know when you book.
How long does a typical detail take?
Most details run around 3.5 hours, though that can vary depending on the service and the condition of the vehicle. We never rush a job — we take whatever time it needs to get it right.
What areas do you service?
We're based in South Jersey and service Cumberland County and surrounding areas within about an hour of Vineland — including Bridgeton, Millville, Woodbury, Glassboro, Hammonton, and more. Not sure if we reach you? Just reach out and we'll let you know.
How much does a detail cost?
Pricing depends on the service and the size and condition of your vehicle. Our packages start at $95 for a Refresh detail and go up from there — Paint Correction starts at $320 and Ceramic Pro starts at $1,150. Add-ons are available starting at $25. DM us or give us a call and we'll get you a quote fast.
What's the difference between a regular wash and a professional detail?
A regular wash might seem like a good alternative but the equipment and chemicals used at most car washes actually cause damage over time — creating swirl marks, water spots, and stripping your paint's protection. A professional detail is backed by years of experience and the right products, which means we're not just cleaning your car, we're protecting and restoring it. It's less of an expense and more of an investment.
Can you detail my car if it's extremely dirty or neglected?
Absolutely — that's exactly what our Disaster Detail package is for. No judgment here, we've seen it all. Heavy pet hair, severe stains, major odors, excessive debris — we handle it all with multiple steam clean passes, ozone and bio bomb odor treatment, and a thorough deep clean from top to bottom. Every disaster detail is quoted individually so reach out and send us some photos and we'll put together a price for you.
Do you detail SUVs, trucks, and vans or just regular cars?
We detail everything — sedans, SUVs, minivans, pickup trucks, work trucks, and fleet vehicles. Pricing may vary by size but no vehicle is too big or too small for us.
How often should I get my car detailed?
A good rule of thumb is to book a detail when you start noticing smells or losing visibility on your mirrors and glass. That said we recommend at least every 2 months to keep your vehicle in the best shape. If you really want to protect your investment we offer maintenance plans that give you significant discounts and keep your car looking its best year round — ask us about it when you book.
Is ceramic coating worth it — and how is it different from wax?
Wax sits on top of your paint and wears off within weeks. Ceramic coating chemically bonds to the surface, creating a hard protective layer that repels water, resists scratches, and blocks UV damage for years — not weeks. It also makes the car significantly easier to maintain between details. If you own a vehicle you care about, ceramic coating is one of the best investments you can make in it. Short answer — yes, it's absolutely worth it.
Will paint correction remove all scratches on my car?
Paint correction removes the vast majority of surface defects — swirl marks, water spots, light scratches, and oxidation. Scratches that have cut through the clear coat down to the base coat or primer can't be fully corrected through polishing alone and may need touch-up paint. We'll always give you an honest assessment before we start so you know exactly what to expect.
How do I book and how fast do you respond?
You can book through our website, call or text us at (856) 538-0407, or reach out through Instagram or Facebook at @SB_AutoWorksNJ. We respond to all inquiries within a couple of minutes.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — SB Autoworks is fully licensed and insured, and backed by 3+ years of professional detailing experience. You can trust that your vehicle is in good hands from start to finish.
Do you offer any discounts?
Absolutely. We offer discounts for fleet clients, maintenance plan members, and customers booking multiple vehicles in one appointment. We also let customers opt out of specific parts of a service they don't need and adjust the price accordingly — including a discount if you supply your own water.
Can I remove parts of a service to lower the price?
Yes — if there's something in a package you don't need, just let us know and we'll remove it and adjust your price. Customers who supply their own water also qualify for a discount on their service.
Do you offer fleet services?
Yes — we work with businesses and individuals with multiple vehicles and offer dedicated fleet pricing. Reach out and we'll build a custom plan around your needs.
What are maintenance plans?
Recurring detail packages that keep your vehicle in top shape year round at a discounted rate. Maintenance clients also get priority scheduling. Ask us about it when you book.
