Why Ceramic Pro

Ceramic coating chemically bonds to your paint's surface, creating a hard, hydrophobic layer that repels water, resists scratches, and blocks UV damage for years rather than weeks — the way a traditional wax does. It also makes ongoing maintenance dramatically easier: dirt and grime slide off instead of grinding in.

Every Ceramic Pro package at SB Autoworks starts with a full paint decontamination and two-stage correction before the coating is ever applied, so you're not sealing swirl marks or oxidation under the protection — you're locking in genuinely corrected paint.

Common question

Is ceramic coating worth it — and how is it different from wax?
Wax sits on top of your paint and wears off within weeks. Ceramic coating chemically bonds to the surface, creating a hard protective layer that repels water, resists scratches, and blocks UV damage for years — not weeks. It also makes the car significantly easier to maintain between details. If you own a vehicle you care about, ceramic coating is one of the best investments you can make in it.
